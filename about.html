OnePlus is a Chinese smartphone company that was founded in 2013 by Pete Lau and Carl Pei. The company's mantra is "Never Settle" and their products are designed to challenge the status quo and market conventions. Here are some things to know about OnePlus:
  • Values: OnePlus' values include empowering the world through better technology and the belief that "good enough is never enough".
  • Products: OnePlus makes premium devices, software, and hardware.
  • Community: OnePlus has an active community that engages with users to co-create the user experience.
  • Ownership: OnePlus is a subsidiary of BBK Electronics, which also owns Oppo, Vivo, and Realme.
  • Presence in India: OnePlus has a large presence in India, with 29% of the market share.
  • Partnership with Google One: OnePlus offers automatic backup, more storage, and other benefits through Google One.
